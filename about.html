Who

I am a statistician working in methodological, computational, and applied statistics, with a focus on functional and longitudinal data analysis.

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at University College Dublin, based in the NexSys (Next Generation Energy Systems) research centre.

My work focuses on developing statistical methodology and implementing computational tools for practical data analysis.
