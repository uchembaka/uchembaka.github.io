04 — Beliefs

Faith

Beliefs

Saved by grace alone through faith in Christ alone, for the glory of God.

I believe that my salvation is by grace alone, received through faith in Jesus Christ alone, and not as a result of my works, so that I may not boast (Ephesians 2:8–9). I believe that Scripture is the final authority over all of my belief and life, and I seek to ground everything I believe in God's Word (2 Timothy 3:16–17).

I believe I am justified freely by His grace through the redemption that is in Christ Jesus (Romans 3:28). I believe there is one mediator between God and humanity, Jesus Christ, who gave Himself as a ransom for all (1 Timothy 2:5–6). Ultimately, all things exist from Him, through Him, and for Him, and therefore all glory belongs to God alone (Romans 11:36).

Saved by grace alone through faith in Christ alone, for the glory of God.
