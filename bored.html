06 — Diversions

Procrastination

A collection of small distractions.

Sudoku

Fill the 9×9 grid so every row, column and box holds 1–9. Three difficulties, with a few gentle hints when you get stuck.

KenKen

A Latin-square cousin of Sudoku. Make each cage reach its target with ×, ÷, +, −. Four sizes, from a quick 4×4 up to a fierce 9×9.

Play →

Tetris

Stack the falling tetrominoes, clear full lines and chase the level counter. Arrows to move and rotate, space to hard-drop.

Memory Matrix

A pattern of squares flashes, then fades — reproduce it from memory. The grid grows each level; three lives, one mistake each.

Snake

Steer the snake to the morsels, growing longer and quicker with each bite. Edges wrap around — only biting your own tail ends it.

Speed Match

A symbol flashes — was it the same as the one just before? Answer fast for 45 seconds; streaks score more, misses cost time.
